Sobre

Trajetória

Graduada em Ciência da Computação na Universidade Federal de Santa Catarina (UFSC). Desenvolvi pesquisas em Robótica Adaptativa e Estratégias Evolutivas no Laboratório de Integração de Sistemas e Aplicações (LISA). About

Background

Computer Science graduate from the Federal University of Santa Catarina (UFSC). I developed research in Adaptive Robotics and Evolutionary Strategies at the Laboratory of Systems Integration and Applications (LISA), and held a PIBIC scholarship in Imitation Learning applied to autonomous vehicles in the Department of Automation and Systems.

My experience includes a PET Computation/Computer Science scholarship at UFSC from 2021–2025, participation in GECCO'22 and GECCO'23 in Portugal, and organization of academic events. I have worked with social media management, promotional design, WordPress maintenance, and committee coordination.
