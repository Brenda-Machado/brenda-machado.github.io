Publicações e Artigos

Trabalhos científicos publicados em conferências, revistas ou workshops

The Effectiveness of Niching on OpenAI-Evolution Strategies in the Evolution of Robotic Behavior

Arthur Holtrup Bianchini, Brenda Silva Machado, Jônata Tyska Carvalho

GECCO'23 Student Workshop - Lisbon, Portugal

O objetivo deste trabalho é analisar como uma adaptação recente das Estratégias Evolutivas desenvolvidas pela OpenAI, que chamamos de OpenAI-ES, se comporta quando trabalha junto com variação ambiental e técnicas de niching. Os resultados mostraram que essa abordagem aplicada ao benchmark de balanceamento de dupla haste pode melhorar a performance em 9% para OpenAI-ES.

Publications and Papers

Scientific works published in conferences, journals or workshops

The Effectiveness of Niching on OpenAI-Evolution Strategies in the Evolution of Robotic Behavior

Arthur Holtrup Bianchini, Brenda Silva Machado, Jônata Tyska Carvalho

GECCO'23 Student Workshop - Lisbon, Portugal

The objective of this work is to analyze how a recent adaptation of Evolution Strategies developed by OpenAI, which we call OpenAI-ES, behaves when working alongside environmental variation and niching techniques. The results showed that this approach applied to the double pole balancing benchmark can improve performance by 9% for OpenAI-ES.

Investigation of the Effectiveness of Niching Techniques and Environmental Differentiation applied to Evolutionary Robotics Algorithms

Brenda Silva Machado, Arthur Holtrup Bianchini, Jônata Tyska Carvalho

REVISTA COMING COMPET'23 - UFSM

This work aims to analyze how the addition of niching techniques, which include environmental differentiation between subpopulations, can be relevant to Evolutionary Robotics algorithms using the version of the ES algorithm recently proposed by OpenAI.
